    • Hey, nice to meet you

    My name's Andrea.

    I'm a junior programmer and ex marketing and advertising professional by trade. I make fun things, like this website, apps and programs, with code.

    In my spare time you'll find me watching AFL, going to the beach, playing mixed netball, going to live music gigs or discovering a new craft sour.

    Whether I'm on the clock or out and about I love discovering patterns, understanding how people think and making the world a safer and more fair place for everyone to live.

    The Journey

    Born

    I've been curious about the world for as long as I can remember. My first word was "more" ... read into that what you will.

    Inspired by Gruen Transfer

    As a teenager I was obsessed with the ABC tv show Gruen. I loved how ads could make you feel something with so few words, or so little time.

    I took this passion and completed a Commerce Degree at UWA majoring in Marketing, Economics and Business Law.

    Got a big girl job

    I was lucky enough to snag an internship, and later a full time job, at one of the big ad agencies in Perth - Meerkats (now called Wunderman Thompson).

    I learnt heaps there about project management, the creative industry and most importantly the art of diplomacy.

    (Remember how I loved Gruen, while working at Meerkats and volunteering with YoungBloods WA I also worked on an ad for "The Pitch" section of the show and won it!)

    Ballet came pirouette-ing into my life

    I took a quick 3 month contract at West Australian Ballet in the Marketing Team.

    Although I kind of fell in love with it and was there for the next 5 years.

    "More" came back into my life

    Around the time COVID-19 hit I started feeling a bit restless at work.

    I got excited by the potential and challenge of learning something new. So I looked into Programming.

    {code} + "hey?";

    I completed a Cert IV in Programming and made some fun stuff - including this site.

    Work in progress

    My career has been a little unusual I guess but it's just a start. Hopefully there's way more to come!
