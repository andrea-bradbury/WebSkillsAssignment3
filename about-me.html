    • Hey, nice to meet you

    I was drawn to the creativity of advertising but soon made my way to ballet, now I use the creativity of advertising, the drive for excellence of ballet and the art of code to make beautifully functional solutions to problems.

    The Journey

    Born

    I've been curious about the world for as long as I can remember. My first word was "more" ... read into that what you will.

    Inspired by Gruen Transfer

    As a teenager I was obsessed with Gruen and advertising. I loved how ads could make you feel something with so few words, or so little time.

    I took this passion and completed a Commerce Degree at UWA.

    Got a big girl job

    I was lucky enough to snag an internship and later a full time job at one of the big ad agencies in Perth - Meerkats (now called Wunderman Thompson).

    I learnt heaps there about project management, the creative industry and most importantly the art of diplomacy.

    Ballet came pirouette-ing into my life

    I took a quick 3 month contract at West Australian Ballet in the Marketing Team.

    Although I kind of fell in love with it and was there for the next 5 years.

    "More" came back into my life

    Around the time COVID-19 hit I started feeling a bit restless.

    I got excited by the potential and challenge of learning something new. So I looked into Programming.

    Code hey...

    I completed a Cert IV in Programming. I made some fun stuff with computer languages - including writing this site.

    Work in progress

    My career has been a little unusual I guess but it's just a start and hopefully there's way more to come!
